# Job Posting: AI Engineer - WatsonX Client Engineering Team

## About IBM

IBM is a global leader in hybrid cloud, AI, and consulting, revolutionizing the way businesses operate through innovative technology solutions. With WatsonX, IBM is at the forefront of AI-powered solutions, empowering enterprises to scale AI with trusted data and governance. Our Client Engineering team collaborates closely with clients to co-create AI-driven innovations that drive business value and transform industries.

## Role Overview

We are seeking a talented and passionate AI Engineer to join our WatsonX Client Engineering team in Bengaluru, India. As an AI Engineer, you will play a pivotal role in building and deploying cutting-edge AI solutions for our enterprise clients, leveraging IBM's expertise in hybrid cloud environments and responsible AI practices.

## Responsibilities

1. Design, develop, and deploy AI models and solutions for enterprise clients, utilizing IBM WatsonX and agentic AI.
2. Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including data scientists, software engineers, and client stakeholders, to co-create AI-driven innovations.
3. Ensure the security, privacy, and ethical use of data in AI projects, adhering to IBM's commitment to responsible AI practices.
4. Stay updated with the latest AI tools, foundation models, and industry trends, contributing to the continuous improvement of our AI solutions.
5. Provide technical guidance and mentorship to junior team members, fostering a culture of learning and collaboration.

## Required Skills and Qualifications

1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or a related field.
2. Proven experience (5+ years) in AI engineering, with a strong background in machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch).
3. Expertise in building, deploying, and maintaining AI models and solutions in production environments.
4. Demonstrated experience working with hybrid cloud environments and enterprise clients.
5. Strong understanding of data governance, security, and privacy in AI projects.
6. Commitment to responsible AI practices and ethical technology use.
7. Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in a multidisciplinary team.
