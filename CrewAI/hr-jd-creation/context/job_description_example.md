# Amazing Job Description Example

## Company Overview
At IBM, we're leading the charge in AI innovation with our WatsonX platform, delivering transformative solutions to clients worldwide. Our Client Engineering team in Bengaluru, India, is pivotal in co-creating AI value with clients, focusing on technology patterns to enhance repeatability and client satisfaction.

## Job Title: AI Engineer – WatsonX Client Engineering

### Location
Bengaluru, India

### Job Summary
As an AI Engineer within IBM's Client Engineering team, you'll collaborate with technical leaders and clients to design, develop, and deploy AI solutions using the WatsonX platform. Your role will involve understanding client requirements, architecting custom AI solutions, and ensuring their seamless integration into client environments.

### Responsibilities
- Design and implement AI and Generative AI solutions to address client-specific business challenges.
- Gather, clean, and prepare large datasets for AI model training.
- Train, fine-tune, evaluate, and optimize AI models to ensure accuracy, performance, and scalability.
- Integrate AI models into cloud-based and on-premises products to enhance workflows and productivity.
- Develop reusable tools and components to standardize and accelerate AI solution development.
- Monitor and maintain deployed models, ensuring consistent performance in production environments.
- Stay updated with the latest AI/ML advancements to enhance product capabilities.
- Effectively communicate technical concepts and strategies to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to leverage IBM tools, driving innovation and alignment.

### Requirements
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- 4+ years of hands-on experience in AI and Machine Learning technologies.
- Proficiency in Python and experience with AI frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Keras.
- Solid understanding of ML/AI concepts, including exploratory data analysis, preprocessing, algorithm selection, and model evaluation.
- Familiarity with Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and Large Language Models (LLMs).
- Experience with LLM frameworks such as Langchain, Langraph, or CrewAI.
- Strong problem-solving skills and a passion for technology.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

### Benefits
- Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off to promote work-life balance.
- Opportunities for continuous learning and professional development.
- Inclusive and dynamic work culture that values diversity.

### How to Apply
Please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your experience and interest in the role to [IBM Careers](https://www.ibm.com/careers/ai-and-watsonx). Include the subject line "AI Engineer – WatsonX Client Engineering Application". We look forward to hearing from you!
